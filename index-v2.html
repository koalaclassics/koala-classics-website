Common Questions

The things buyers ask first

Do you handle my payment? +

No. Every payment is made directly between you and the seller. Koala Classics never holds or handles your vehicle purchase funds — that keeps you in control and removes the biggest risk in an overseas buy.

How do I know this is legitimate? +

We're a registered Australian business (ABN 29 820 791 391) running a transparent, step-by-step process where you stay in control of the transaction the entire way.

Can I get the car inspected before I commit? +

Yes — and we recommend it. We coordinate an independent third-party inspection and you receive the full report before you decide.

What makes a car eligible to import? +

The U.S. 25-year rule exempts vehicles 25 years or older from federal safety (NHTSA) and emissions (EPA) standards. You complete the EPA 3520-1 and DOT HS-7 exemption forms at the port of entry — no physical modifications required under federal law. State registration rules vary, so always check your state.

How long does the whole process take? +

It varies by vehicle availability, inspection, and shipping schedules — typically a few months from sourcing to delivery. We keep you updated at every stage.

Do you guarantee import approval? +

No. We provide guidance and coordination, but the buyer is responsible for complying with federal and state import laws. We point you to exactly what you need.
