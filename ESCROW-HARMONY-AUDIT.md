## 5. PROCESS FLOW HARMONY

### Transaction Flow (Now Consistent Across All Pages)

```
1. Buyer requests quote
   ↓
2. Koala Classics sources vehicles
   ↓
3. Buyer negotiates with seller directly (NO PAYMENT YET)
   ↓
4. Independent inspection (buyer sees full report)
   ↓
5. Buyer approves & initiates payment
   ↓
6. Buyer sends funds to ESCROW (not to seller)
   ↓
7. Koala Classics coordinates export
   ↓
8. Vehicle arrives in USA
   ↓
9. Buyer verifies vehicle condition
   ↓
10. Buyer instructs escrow to release funds to seller
   ↓
11. Koala Classics finalizes customs/registration
```
